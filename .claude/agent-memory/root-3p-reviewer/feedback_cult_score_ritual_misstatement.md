Community Cult guides frequently misstate the Score ritual by saying "score all matching-suit gardens" or implying the VP award is per-garden. The correct mechanic (`rule:10.5.3`): reveal and spend a card to score the VP printed above the **rightmost empty Gardens space** of the matching suit — a single fixed amount determined by track position, not a sum of individual gardens. Having more gardens on the map advances the track (higher VP per Score), but each Score ritual pays once per suit, not once per garden.

**Why:** This misstatement appeared in the makecraftgame--lizard-cult-guide.md review (2026-04-29). The phrase "score all matching-suit gardens" conflates the prerequisite (must have at least one garden of the suit on map) with the payment (track-value VP).

**How to apply:** Flag any Cult source that says scoring "per garden," "all gardens," or implies VP scales additively with garden count in a per-garden way. The track-value model is what the Law defines.

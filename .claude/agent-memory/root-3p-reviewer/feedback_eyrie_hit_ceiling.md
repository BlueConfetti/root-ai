Commander leader (`rule:7.8.3`): +1 extra hit as attacker.
Maximum dice roll: 3.
Commander alone: 4 max hits.

To reach 5 hits: Commander + Brutal Tactics (`card:ROOT-58 (Base Game)`) simultaneously.

**Why:** Community sources sometimes cite "five possible hits" as a baseline Eyrie capability. It is achievable but situational.
**How to apply:** Flag this claim in any source that presents 5 hits as baseline rather than maximum-with-cards.

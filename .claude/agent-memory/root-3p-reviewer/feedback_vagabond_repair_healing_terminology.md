Community sources (especially pre-2021) often say Vagabond should "heal" items. The correct game terms are:
- **Repair** (`rule:9.5.7`): exhaust one `item:hammer` to move one damaged item to Satchel in its current orientation.
- **Rest** (`rule:9.6.1`): if in a forest at Evening, all damaged items move to Satchel face up (free full repair).

The advice to "avoid frequent healing" (as in elusivemeeple--root-strategy-tips.md) directly contradicts the project's Vagabond profile, which lists "Neglecting Repair" as a common pitfall.

Also: community sources sometimes say "caves" when they mean **ruins** — the correct term for the four ruin slots on the map (`rule:9.3.4`).

**Why:** These are beginner-oriented sources written before the project's faction profiles established vetted guidance. The Repair-vs-heal conflation leads to incorrectly valuing the mechanic.
**How to apply:** When reviewing Vagabond sources, flag "healing" as non-standard. Cross-check any heal-rarely or heal-often advice against the Vagabond profile's Common Pitfalls section.

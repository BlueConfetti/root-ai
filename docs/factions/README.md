# Root Faction Profiles

Comprehensive in-depth profiles for each Root faction, written from the perspective of playing that faction. Each profile follows the 12-section spine defined in [`../superpowers/specs/2026-04-28-faction-profiles-design.md`](../superpowers/specs/2026-04-28-faction-profiles-design.md): At a Glance, Win Condition, Setup, Faction Rules & Abilities, Turn Structure, Scoring Engine, Playbook, Threat Factions, Card Priorities, Common Pitfalls, Mechanic Clarifications, Reference Index.

Profiles are p16-only (October 2025 Law). Mechanical claims cite into `rules.yml` and `faq.yml` per the conventions in the project [`CLAUDE.md`](../../CLAUDE.md). Strategy claims sourced from community discussion are flagged with caveats.

For opponent-facing distilled advice (how to help or hurt each faction), see the [help/hurt cheatsheet](../root-faction-help-hurt-cheatsheet.md).

## Base Game

- [Marquise de Cat](marquise.md) — Industrial cat empire scoring through sustained building construction; wood-supply chain across ruled clearings is the engine.
- [Eyrie Dynasties](eyrie.md) — Birds locked into the ever-growing Decree; score per roost at Evening, but unfulfillable columns trigger turmoil and Humiliate VP losses.
- [Woodland Alliance](woodland.md) — Underground guerrillas converting opponent moves and removals into Supporters; score through sympathy spread, base placement, and revolts.
- [Vagabond](vagabond.md) — Solo character crafting items, completing quests, and managing relationships; supports standard, dominance, and coalition victory paths.

## Riverfolk Expansion

- [Lizard Cult](cult.md) — Sermons and rituals converting Lost Souls into acolytes; score by performing conspiracies in Outcast clearings.
- [Riverfolk Company](riverfolk.md) — Mercantile otter alliance selling services for funds; score through trade posts and dividends.

## Underworld Expansion

- [Underground Duchy](duchy.md) — Mole society expanding from the Burrow via tunnels; score by swaying ministers as the Duchy's standing rises.
- [Corvid Conspiracy](corvid.md) — Hidden plot tokens (bomb, snare, raid, extortion) scoring on revelation and exposure pressure.

## Marauder Expansion

- [Lord of the Hundreds](warlord.md) — Wrathful warlord stack scoring through Oppress, Wrath, and crowned mood-driven aggression keyed off the warlord's clearing.
- [Keepers in Iron](keepers-iron.md) — Knight company recovering relics from forests and delivering them to waystations to score.

## Homeland Expansion

- [Lilypad Diaspora](diaspora.md) — Three-tribe frog civic-minded faction scoring through Lilypad-track development.
- [Twilight Council](council.md) — Civic deliberation faction scoring through assemblies passed in clearings they rule.
- [Knaves of the Deepwood](knaves.md) — Forest-dwelling skunk Captains taking Prisoners and filching items; score through acclaim and item plays.
